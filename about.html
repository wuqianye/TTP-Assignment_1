Pet Adoption

Every year, nearly three million pets are euthanized in the United States due to an abundance of pets in animal shelters and too few resources able to take care of all of them. Furthermore, the current pandemic has made matters worse. Less people are going to animal shelters and as a result, animal shelters are becoming more and more overpopulated. Adopting an animal will really change their life and change your own. One day you'll look back and realize it was one of the best decisions you ever made.

    Reasons to Adopt a Pet today
  1. Adopting a pet saves their life as well as opens up room for another animal at the shelter
  2. Everyone deserves a second chance, especially for some of these animals
  3. Adopting an adult pet has many benefits, most of them know tricks already such as "sit" and "fetch"
  4. Instead of paying hundreds of dollars for purebred puppies or kittens, adopting a pet is has a much lower cost
  5. Having a pet can significantly improve your daily life, having a pet means more walks and talking to all the other animal owners make you more social
  6. Adopting from an animal or rescue shelter is helpful since you know what you are getting because the group has a history on the animal characteristics
  7. When your friends ask where your adorable pet is from, you can tell them “at the shelter.” This adoption may convince your friends and others to follow your footsteps and adopt
  8. You'll change a homeless animal's entire world and make it better indefinitely
